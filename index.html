ABOUT US

QUANTA is a part of departmental association, the main motto of quanta is to develop a hands on technical knowledge that encourages a person to move on with logical aptitude and to have a good command over Robotics & Embedded system.

DEPARTMENTS

Technical

The technical department is a group of young technocrat minds where we learn stuff like IoT/embedded systems/drone making, basically everything tech! And then apply them to real life projects.

Web Development

The Web Development department of QUANTA handles the official website of the society. A group of people who learn the nuances of the web together and take on new projects!

Design

We look up to the design department of our society every now and then when we need posters for our workshops. A group of people who know Adobe and Corel in and out!
